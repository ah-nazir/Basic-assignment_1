Homemade Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (not too hot)
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (e.g. pepperoni, mushrooms, olives, bell peppers, onions, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit for about 5 minutes or until the mixture becomes foamy.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil to the bowl. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Place the dough on a floured surface and knead for about 10 minutes or until the dough is smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a large, greased bowl, cover with a damp towel, and let it rise in a warm, draft-free area for 1-2 hours or until the dough has doubled in size.
  5. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  6. Roll out the dough on a floured surface into your desired shape and thickness.
  7. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  8. Spread the tomato sauce over the dough, leaving about 1/2 inch of space around the edges.
  9. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top of the tomato sauce.
  10. Add your desired toppings, making sure not to overload the pizza.
  11. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the toppings.
  12. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and the crust is golden brown.
  13. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, slice, and enjoy your homemade pizza!
